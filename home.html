2021 Orange County NY Democrat for Legislative District 6

I am highly-qualified and equally as passionate to serve as the Orange County Legislator for District 6, representing both the Town and City of Newburgh. My longevity and service to the Newburgh community combined with my long term administrative experience in Public Education, Labor Administration, and Public Service has enabled me to be keenly aware of the challenges and complexities confronting our citizens, parents, homeowners, for-profit and non-profit organizations, businesses, social and spiritual organizations, and government leaders.

Yet as I examine and observe our wonderful community, I believe that we undeniably have the means, the resources, the wherewithal and most importantly the desire to make everyday life for the people of Newburgh better. It is my goal to provide the leadership necessary for us to succeed in all areas of community life.

We can accomplish this through targeted planning and infrastructure development, meaningful environmental and quality of life improvements, goal-oriented economic education, training and employment opportunities and safe, extracurricular, recreational and academic developmental opportunities for our children.

With your support, I look forward to providing highly-effective leadership as your representative in the Orange County Legislature.

Experienced, Insightful Leadership

Roger Ramjug has been a resident of Newburgh for over 50 years. He is a native from Trinidad and Tobago and migrated to the United States in 1969 where he was assigned to the Saint Cabrini Children Home in Highland. He became a foster child to the Prokosch family. Today, he is married with four children and is the Director of Management and Efficiency in the Newburgh Enlarged City School District, which is his alma mater. He is also a United States Air Force Veteran who worked on flight line refueling. He earned his Undergraduate degree at the National Labor College in Maryland in Labor Administration and Labor Education.

He earned a Masters in Public Administration. Later on, he earned his second Masters In Education Administration at SUNY New Paltz and recently graduated this month with a CAS in education Business Administration from SUNY Buffalo...
