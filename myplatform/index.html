My Platform

I consider myself qualified and equally as passionate to serve as the Orange County Legislator for District 6, representing both the Town and City of Newburgh. My longevity and service to the Newburgh community combined with my long term administrative experience in Public Education, Labor Administration, and Public Service has enabled me to be keenly aware of the challenges and complexities confronting our citizens, parents, homeowners, for-profit and non-profit organizations, businesses, social and spiritual organizations, and government leaders.

Yet as I examine and observe our wonderful community, I believe that we undeniably have the means, the resources, the wherewithal and most importantly the desire to make everyday life for the people of Newburgh better. It is my goal to provide the leadership necessary for us to succeed in all areas of community life.

We can accomplish this through targeted planning and infrastructure development, meaningful environmental and quality of life improvements, goal-oriented economic and employment opportunities and safe, extracurricular, recreational and academic developmental opportunities for our children.

With your support, I look forward to providing highly-effective leadership as your representative in the Orange County Legislature.

2021 Orange County NY Legislative District 6 Candidate
